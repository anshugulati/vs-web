INSIDE H&M

H&M Studio Capsule

An evening collection designed at our Stockholm atelier.

14 November 2024

The festive season presents multiple occasions to dress up and the latest Studio capsule offers sleek and sophisticated looks that are ideal for celebratory moments.

“This season, the design team channeled quiet confidence, creating after-dark pieces that set pulses racing, while evoking a sense of ease,” says Kathrin Deutsch, collection designer at H&M Studio.

The collection is rooted in refined tailoring and feminine draping with a modern twist, resulting in eveningwear that transcends time and trend.

“We distilled sharp silhouettes and edgy details in ways that feel effortless to wear this party season, combining them with covetable accessories that spark desire,” says Ann-Sofie Johansson, creative advisor and head of womenswear design at H&M.

Key looks include an expertly tailored navy suit, a sweeping black leather coat, a champagne-hued gown, day-to-night denim and a padded leather bomber jacket. Accessories enhance the collection with subtle sophistication in the form of a gold leather bag, heeled leather booties, gold-tipped loafers and rhinestone-studded jewellery.

An exclusive edit of pieces from the collection will be available to rent at selected stores in Stockholm, Berlin, Amsterdam and London.

Launching in selected stores and online 21 November.
